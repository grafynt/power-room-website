This Year's Theme

GRACE OVERFLOWING

"And God is able to bless you abundantly, so that in all things at all times, having all that you need, you will abound in every good work."  — 2 Corinthians 9:8

This year, we step into a season marked by overflow — not just receiving grace, but becoming a channel of it. When grace runs deep in our hearts, it spills outward: into our friendships, our families, our city. Power Room 2026 is a call to live so rooted in God's undeserved kindness that it becomes the way we love, give, lead, and grow — together.

Who We Are

MORE THAN
A COMMUNITY

We're a family. A home. A place where real life meets real faith.

Read our story tap to open

We are a youth community under GBI SLCC @gbi.slcc — a home for young people who are learning to walk with God in the middle of real life.

We believe church is not just a place you go to, but a family you grow with. We laugh together, we worship together, we learn together, and most importantly — we grow together. We are not perfect, but we are committed to pursuing a perfect God, side by side.

Our heart is to see a generation rooted in Christ, confident in their identity, and bold in their purpose.

That's why we don't just gather on Sundays — we also have small groups (HOST), where we grow deeper, share honestly, pray for one another, and build real friendships that last beyond Sunday.

If you're looking for a community, a safe space, or simply a place to start again — maybe this is your sign. ✦

Our Values
GLEAM
God-centered  ·  Loving  ·  Engage  ·  Authentic  ·  Maturity

Five words. One identity. This is who we are
and who we're becoming — together.

G
God-Centered

Everything we do starts and ends with God first. Our decisions, our direction, our community — all rooted in Him. A God-centered life isn't just going to church; it's carrying His presence into every room you walk into.

L
Loving

Love is not just a feeling — it's a daily choice. We choose to love people the way Christ loves us: unconditionally, sacrificially, and without judgment. Everyone is welcome here, exactly as they are.

E
Engage

Faith is not a spectator sport. We show up — for real conversations, for events that build community, for the world around us. We engage because we believe every interaction matters and every person deserves to be seen.

A
Authentic

No masks. No performance. Just real. We create space to be honest about our doubts, our struggles, and our stories — because authenticity is what allows true community to form. You don't have to have it all together to belong here.

M
Maturity

We are committed to growing — not just in knowledge, but in character, wisdom, and our walk with God. Maturity isn't a destination; it's the daily decision to become more like Christ, one step at a time, together.
