Who We Are

MORE THAN
A COMMUNITY

We're a family. A home. A place where real life meets real faith.

Read our story tap to open

We are a youth community under GBI SLCC @gbi.slcc — a home for young people who are learning to walk with God in the middle of real life.

We believe church is not just a place you go to, but a family you grow with. We laugh together, we worship together, we learn together, and most importantly — we grow together. We are not perfect, but we are committed to pursuing a perfect God, side by side.

Our heart is to see a generation rooted in Christ, confident in their identity, and bold in their purpose.

That's why we don't just gather on Sundays — we also have small groups (HOST), where we grow deeper, share honestly, pray for one another, and build real friendships that last beyond Sunday.

If you're looking for a community, a safe space, or simply a place to start again — maybe this is your sign. ✦

Our Values
GLEAM
God-centered  ·  Loving  ·  Engage  ·  Authentic  ·  Maturity

Five words. One identity. This is who we are
and who we're becoming — together.

G
God-Centered

Everything we do starts and ends with God first. Our decisions, our direction, our community — all rooted in Him. A God-centered life isn't just going to church; it's carrying His presence into every room you walk into.

L
Loving

Love is not just a feeling — it's a daily choice. We choose to love people the way Christ loves us: unconditionally, sacrificially, and without judgment. Everyone is welcome here, exactly as they are.

E
Engage

Faith is not a spectator sport. We show up — for real conversations, for events that build community, for the world around us. We engage because we believe every interaction matters and every person deserves to be seen.

A
Authentic

No masks. No performance. Just real. We create space to be honest about our doubts, our struggles, and our stories — because authenticity is what allows true community to form. You don't have to have it all together to belong here.

M
Maturity

We are committed to growing — not just in knowledge, but in character, wisdom, and our walk with God. Maturity isn't a destination; it's the daily decision to become more like Christ, one step at a time, together.

FAQ

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

Pertanyaan yang sering ditanyakan tentang Power Room, ibadah di Gading Serpong, dan HOST small groups.

Dimana lokasi gereja GBI SLCC Gading Serpong?
GBI SLCC berlokasi di OnePM Building, lantai 5, Gading Serpong, Tangerang. Power Room (komunitas pemuda) ibadah di lantai yang sama setiap Minggu pukul 16.00 WIB. Pintu terbuka pukul 15.30.
Ibadah Power Room jam berapa?
Ibadah Power Room diadakan setiap Minggu pukul 16.00 WIB. Pintu terbuka pukul 15.30 — datang lebih awal biar dapat tempat duduk yang enak dan sempat ngobrol sebelum ibadah mulai!
Apa itu HOST di Power Room?
HOST adalah small group (kelompok kecil) di Power Room — tempat anggota komunitas berkumpul setiap minggu dalam kelompok kecil untuk beribadah, belajar Firman, dan saling mendukung. HOST adalah inti kehidupan komunitas Power Room, bukan sekadar kegiatan tambahan. Cari tahu lebih lanjut di section HOST.
Apakah ada komunitas pemuda Kristen di Gading Serpong?
Ya! Power Room adalah komunitas pemuda GBI SLCC yang aktif di Gading Serpong, Tangerang. Kami berkumpul setiap Minggu pukul 16.00 dan memiliki HOST small groups yang aktif setiap minggunya. Komunitas kami terbuka untuk semua anak muda di area Gading Serpong dan sekitarnya.
Apakah Power Room cocok untuk yang baru percaya atau belum pernah ke gereja?
Banget! Power Room terbuka untuk semua orang — yang baru percaya, yang sedang mencari, maupun yang sudah lama berjalan dengan Tuhan. Tidak ada standar atau syarat tertentu. Semua disambut apa adanya.
Bagaimana cara bergabung dengan Power Room?
Caranya gampang: datang ke ibadah Minggu pukul 16.00 di OnePM Building lantai 5, Gading Serpong. Kamu juga bisa follow Instagram @power.slcc atau cek linktr.ee/power.slcc untuk info lebih lanjut.
Apa tema Power Room tahun 2026?
Tema Power Room 2026 adalah "Grace Overflowing" — berdasarkan 2 Korintus 9:8. Tahun ini kami melangkah dalam musim yang ditandai dengan kelimpahan kasih karunia: bukan hanya menerimanya, tetapi menjadi salurannya bagi orang-orang di sekitar kita.
